Tips to find the best Real Estate Services in India

Here are some practical tips to follow while looking for an online real estate service in India:

Reputation Take your time, explore and find out what others have to say about a real estate platform. Their feedback and response will help you gauge the credibility of the platform.

Checking Credentials: Ensure that the real estate agency or agent is registered with the local real estate regulatory authority, such as RERA (Real Estate Regulatory Authority). This registration confirms their legitimacy and also makes it responsible for any customer grievances.

Expertise: Pay attention to the platform’s core competencies. Check for the services that they offer the best, and also try to take a look at the team of experts working with them.

Personalised Services: Choose a real estate service that offers personalised attention to your specific needs. A good agent should be willing to understand your preferences, budget, and other requirements closely.

Ease of Use: For a digital platform to be good, it has to be simple to use. Navigating their online platform should come intuitively to users, and at the same time, there should be simultaneous offline channels for communication as well.
